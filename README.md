# CHS Inc. (chs)

CHS Inc. is a Fortune 500 diversified, integrated, global agricultural cooperative owned by farmers, ranchers, cooperatives, and individual producers. Headquartered in Inver Grove Heights, Minnesota, CHS is the largest farmer-owned cooperative in the United States with 750+ member cooperatives, 75,000+ individual producer-owners, and 20,000+ preferred stockholders, serving customers in 65 countries.
